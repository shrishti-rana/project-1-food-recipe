Pav Bhaji

Ingredients


  • For Bhaji:

    Potato, cauliflower, peas, capsicum, oil/butter, cumin seeds, chopped onion, ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, pav bhaji masala, salt, butter, coriander leaves, lemon.

  • For Pav:

    Pav buns, butter.

Recipe

  1. Bhaji:

    Boil and mash vegetables. Heat butter, add cumin & onion. Add ginger-garlic & tomato puree. Add spices and mashed vegetables. Cook well, add butter, lemon & coriander.

  2. Pav:

    Toast pav with butter on tawa till golden.

    3. Serve Hot with onion, lemon & extra butter üòç
